About Us

CV Bali TUI (Bali Trans Utama International) is a professional logistics and cargo company located in Gianyar, Bali. We specialize in international freight forwarding and provide reliable and efficient shipping solutions for individuals and businesses. With a team of dedicated experts and global partners, we ensure that your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time. Whether you're exporting products from Bali or receiving international shipments, we offer full-service support from documentation to final delivery.
